--- layout: page title: An overview of FAO dataset --- FAO dataset is a source full of data and it can lead to many kinds of insights. We decided to perform our project on production in agriculture and live stocks. In first instance we needed to study data among several countries. This choropleth summarizes data cleaning we made it shows the world situation in the last 20 years. Since we were dealing with data related to huge amount of production, we decided, for each country, to compute the percentage of the production over the all world. We made this for many indicators, below you can find the four more important: Crops Production, Livestock Production, Emission related to Crops, Emission related to Livestock.
